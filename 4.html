Final Year Project

Did a final year project, which is the smart glasses for elderly people in this project
is to Alzheimer (Stage 2) and elderly people to remind about the frequently forgotten things.
A Wearable glass issued where the camera is fixed in the frame,
and it is connected to the local server with the help of router. In each room a Bluetooth beacon is fixed,
when the user enters the room, the UID of the particular Bluetooth is noted
and those things in the roomare upload to the cloud
and classified under the particular room name.
The database information is sent to the app ,where the user can view the thing.
